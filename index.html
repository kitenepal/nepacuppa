Tamp Culture Coffee

Where: Gun Street
The independent coffee kiosk, which is very popular with Reading caffeine fans, recently upgraded from a cart to something a bit more permanent.
Tamp Culture launched cart just outside The Oracle in Gun Street in April 2014.
The team specialises in serving smooth flat whites, lattes and cappuccinos in Reading.
The kiosk is open Monday to Friday from 8am to 6pm, Saturday from 9am to 6pm and Sunday from 11am to 5pm.
